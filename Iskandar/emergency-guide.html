Emergency Guide

Essential safety procedures and emergency response protocols for maritime situations.

🚨

Emergency Response

Immediate actions to take in life-threatening maritime emergencies.

  • Stay calm and assess the situation
  • Activate emergency beacon or radio
  • Contact nearest coast guard immediately
  • Follow "Mayday" distress call protocol
  • Prepare life jackets and safety equipment
⚠️

Weather Emergencies

Handling sudden weather changes and storm conditions.

  • Monitor weather forecasts regularly
  • Prepare for sudden squalls and storms
  • Secure all loose equipment on deck
  • Reduce speed and head for sheltered waters
  • Keep emergency supplies readily accessible
🏊

Swimming Safety

Essential guidelines for safe swimming and water activities.

  • Never swim alone - use buddy system
  • Check water conditions before entering
  • Be aware of rip currents and undertows
  • Wear appropriate safety gear
  • Know your swimming limits and abilities
🏥

Medical Emergencies

Basic first aid and medical response procedures.

  • Carry comprehensive first aid kit
  • Know basic CPR and rescue techniques
  • Treat injuries immediately and properly
  • Seek professional medical help when needed
  • Document all medical incidents
📡

Communication

Maintaining contact and signaling for help.

  • Keep communication devices charged
  • Learn international distress signals
  • Register trip plans with authorities
  • Use proper radio communication protocols
  • Have backup communication methods
🛟

Equipment Safety

Proper use and maintenance of safety equipment.

  • Regularly inspect all safety gear
  • Know how to use life-saving equipment
  • Keep emergency supplies well-stocked
  • Test equipment functionality regularly
  • Replace expired or damaged equipment
