About BeachGuard

Committed to maritime safety through innovative technology and community-driven solutions.

Our Mission

BeachGuard was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make maritime activities safer for everyone. We believe that access to accurate, real-time information about water conditions should be available to all boaters, swimmers, and water enthusiasts.

Real-Time Data

We provide accurate, up-to-the-minute information about tides, currents, weather conditions, and safety zones to help you make informed decisions on the water.

Community Driven

Our platform connects users to share real-time reports and observations, creating a collaborative network of maritime safety information.

Innovation First

We leverage cutting-edge technology including AI, AR, and IoT to deliver innovative safety solutions that adapt to your needs.
