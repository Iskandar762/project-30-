Understanding Safety Zones

Safety zones are designated areas where swimming, boating, or other water activities are considered safe based on current conditions, tides, currents, and environmental factors. BeachGuard provides real-time updates on these zones to help you make informed decisions.

✓

Safe Zones

Areas where conditions are optimal for water activities. These zones are monitored for water quality, currents, and weather conditions.

!

Caution Zones

Areas requiring extra attention. Conditions may include moderate currents, changing tides, or light winds that could affect safety.

✕

Danger Zones

Areas where water activities are not recommended. Strong currents, high waves, poor visibility, or hazardous conditions exist.
